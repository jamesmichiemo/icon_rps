Font Awesome Rock Paper Scissors

This is a Rock Paper Scissors game implemented with Font Awesome icons for all of the graphics. It was created by Morgan Laco and girlfriend Jocelyn in May 2016. We hope you like it, and if you have any feedback, check out the repo

How to Play

Click on one of the three icons ( ) at the bottom of the screen to select your throw. The hand on the left will make the throw you choose. You will see , , or indicating that you won, lost, or tied. Every win will grow your booty, the fancy icons at the top.
